### Key Features

- **Typing Practice Gameplay**

  - Enter English or programming sentences within a time limit
  - Correct input = player attacks, mistakes or timeout = monster attacks

- **Difficulty & Battle System**

  - Difficulty affects monster types, spawn rates, item drops, and boss patterns
  - Three monster types (Ghost, Skeleton, Rabbit), each with unique stats and skills
  - Boss battle combines movement, jumping, shooting, and problem-solving

- **Items & Stat Growth**

  - Defeated monsters drop one of five random reward boxes
  - Rewards enhance stats such as life, attack, and defense

- **Game Interface & Animation**
  - Visual effects for attacks, damage, death, rewards, status window, timer, etc.
  - Real-time feedback for correct and incorrect typing

### Role

- **Full Game Design & Implementation**
  - Console screen control with ASCII art
  - Monster/boss logic and difficulty system design
  - Implementation of player stats, item drops, and combat interactions
