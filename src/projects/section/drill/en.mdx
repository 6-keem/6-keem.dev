### Key Features

- **Real-time Job Listings**

  - Display the latest job postings using the Employment24 API
  - Save favorite companies and track trends

- **Company Analysis & Resume Review Support**

  - Analyze company IR materials to provide funding flow and latest trends
  - Write and revise resumes based on analysis results

- **AI Mock Interviews**

  - Conduct realistic mock interviews based on company analysis and resume content
  - Provide scores and feedback after the interview

- **Integrated Job Preparation**
  - Search job postings, analyze companies, write resumes, and take AI mock interviews all in one iOS app

### Tech Stack

- **Platform**: iOS (Swift)
- **AI/LLM**: AI-based analysis and interview simulation
- **API Integration**: Employment24 API (job listings)
- **Infrastructure**: Cloud-based service operation

### Role

- **Project Planning & Development**
  - Manage project schedule and team roles
  - Implement iOS app screens and functionalities using Swift
- **AI Service Planning & Integration**
  - Apply AI models for company analysis and interview features
  - Design job API integration and AI mock interview logic

### Expected Impact

- Enable job seekers to **experience the entire job preparation process in one place**, maximizing preparation efficiency
- Provide **personalized support** through company analysis and resume review
- Enhance **real-world readiness** with AI mock interviews and real-time feedback
- Improve **user convenience** by integrating complex job preparation steps
